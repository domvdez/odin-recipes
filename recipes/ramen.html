Ramen

Description:

Broke college students rejoice! Instant ramen can be made a little bit better with a couple of extra ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Instant Ramen
  • Egg
  • Green Onion
  • Pork Belly

Steps:

Follow the regular steps you'd follow when cooking instant ramen (add noods to boiling water for 3 minutes, then add the seasoning packet) but add the egg during the boiling process.

Cook pork belly separately on a pan and make sure it's cooked to 165 degrees farenheit.

Once the ramen is done, pour it into a bowl, add the pork belly on top and give it a bit of color with a hefty handful of chopped green onion.
