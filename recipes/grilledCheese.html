Grilled Cheese

Description

Ooey gooey grilled cheese can easily be made with the combo of 3 things:

  1. bread
  2. cheese (melty ones preferred)
  3. butter (altough I've beed using mayo lately)

Next, all you do is put the cheese in between two slices of bread, throw butter on a hot pan and grill it!

Make sure to flip it to get both sides nice and golden brown!
